CRUDE OIL PRICE PREDICTION


Demand for oil is inelastic,therefore the rise in price is good news for producers because they will see an increase in their revenue.Oil importers,however will experience increased cost of purchasing oil.Because oil is the largest traded commodity,the effects are quit significant.A rising oil price can even shift economic/pollitical power from oil importers to oil exporters.The crude oil price movements are subject to diverse influencing factors.
